What we can do for you
Procurement

Sourcing

We simply do not believe that business owners should waste so much time trying to understand how to source effectively from Chinese Companies. So we started a movement to make expert sourcing help available to all. Get quality products, never leave money on the table, and don’t expose yourself or clients to risk.

Warehousing

We offer unlimited warehouse storage space with state-of-the-art technology and security both in China and Ghana. Warehouses need to perform ‘smarter’ by operating as efficiently as possible, reducing error rates and adhering to best practices.


Sea Freight

Is the best option for bulk goods transportation, sometimes it’s the only option when importing from China. Low prices, large volumes, FCL or LCL options; all these advantages make delivery by sea a first choice for most international trades from China to Ghana.

Air Freight

We handle your air freight parcel delivery with the professionalism and thoroughness. No matter the size of your shipment, We are committed to getting it where it needs to go. And we handle your parcel delivery like no other logistics company — thoroughly and professionally

Secured Payment

Don’t let payment, excessive bank charges or transfer limits obstruct your business with Chinese suppliers. We can assist you to make instant payments to your Chinese suppliers through bank transfer, Alipay & even QQ while you pay us in cedis. Whatever the amount, we offer the best rates and lowest charges.
